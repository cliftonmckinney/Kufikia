How it works

  • Start with a team

    Sign up and you'll be assigned to a cohort made up of ten total students. You'll spend the next nine weeks working together through an online curriculum developed by experts in the field.

  • We'll bring in a sponsor

    Each cohort is sponsored by an amazing company. We have lots of companies on board as sponsors, many among the most well-known startups in the world.

  • They'll bring along mentors

    Your sponsor company pays for the program, but they also provide something much more valuable: help when you need it. Each cohort will have five mentors from the sponsor company who will check your deliverables and answer questions from you.

  • We'll give you lots of motivation

    Most folks *want* to learn a new technology, and many even get started, but most never finish. To prevent this from happening, we've kept the team size small and given you mentors for support.

  • when you finish, you'll be rewarded

    And there's something more. If (when) you finish the program, you'll split $1,000 equally among the graduating teammates. If you all make it, that's $100 apiece. Tough it out when others don't and the reward is even greater.
