How Kufikia Works

  • Our platform

    Kufikia is an online, mentor-driven learning platform for experienced developers. We take what you're already doing--learning new technologies on your own--and kick it up a notch.

  • structure

    Each session lasts nine weeks, and every three weeks you'll complete a mini-project. The goal is understanding and competence. By the end of the nine weeks, you will have built three increasingly complex apps and have a solid understanding of the technology.

  • study buddies

    You'll go through your session with nine other students. You'll be studying the same material at the same time, and you'll have the ability to communicate and help one another. We typically group students by region, so there's even an opportunity (if you choose) to meet in person.

  • support

    You'll have the opportunity to work with expert mentors from amazing sponsor companies. Once a week, they'll join the group and answer questions you weren't able to figure out on your own.
