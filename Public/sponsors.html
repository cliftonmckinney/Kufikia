Why sponsor a Kufikia cohort?

Great software developers are always learning. In fact, forget about developers. Great people are always learning.

Helping people learn is awesome. It's something great companies should want to do regardless of the direct or indirect benefits. But helping people learn can also be a great opportunity.

Kufikia gives you a first-hand look into how experienced developers interact with your team. That direct interaction blows the traditional interview out of the water.

Help people learn. Find employees who fit. Become a Kufikia sponsor.

How it works

  • We evaluate and filter each program applicant

    Kufikia is not for beginners. In fact, only about 50% of our applicants get in. We find smart, ambitious developers who already have experience with one or more languages. They'll know how to code before they start the program.

  • We Pair accepted participants with amazing companies

    Accepted applicants are placed into ten person cohorts based on geographic region and other factors. Each cohort spends nine weeks learning a new technology, and during that time, they are mentored by representatives from three amazing companies.

  • Sponsors recruit the graduates who fit their organization

    More than 85% of applicants (so far) will entertain new options with sponsor companies that fit them well. Odds are great that as a sponsor you'll be able to find graduates who fit your organization perfectly and can contribute from day one. That is worth the effort.

Be a part of the Kufikia family

We'd love for you to become a part of the Kufikia family. You'll be helping developers learn new things, you'll be helping a significant number of them escape jobs they don't love, and you'll even get the chance to hire some awesome new teammates. Interested? Sign up now.
